Continuous Improvement Process

CIP stands for Continuous Improvement Process and represents the method by which the management continually improves suitability, adequacy and effectiveness.

Reasons to use the tool:
  • Using this tool, you can improve your job and make it easier.
  • It makes people accountable. When continuous improvement permeates all levels of your organization, it stops being one person’s job and becomes part of everyone’s job.
  • You will find that once you have established rules and guidelines for your continuous improvement, it will be easier to include it in all aspects of your job.
  • Your collaborators and employees will benefit from the improvements and increased dynamism that continuous improvement provides.
  • The best ideas are rewarded every year in December.
